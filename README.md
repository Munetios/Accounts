# Munetios Accounts

Munetios Accounts is the central authentication system for the Munetios ecosystem.  
It allows users to securely sign in and manage their identity across Munetios apps and services.

Munetios Accounts is currently **coming soon**.

---

## Overview

Munetios Accounts will provide a unified login system for all Munetios products, allowing users to access services with one secure account.

It is designed with a **privacy-first architecture**, minimal data collection, and strong security protections.

---

## Supported Services (Planned)

Munetios Accounts will integrate with many Munetios services, including:

- Munetios AI
- Munetios Search
- Munetios OmniWrite
- Munetios Maps
- Munetios Tasks
- Munetios Notebook
- Munetios Drive
- Munetios Apps Hub
- Munetios Hosting Platform

## Key Features (Planned)

- Secure authentication
- Privacy-focused design
- Single sign-on across Munetios services
- Encrypted user data
- Secure session management

---

## Privacy

Munetios Accounts follows strong privacy principles:

- Minimal data collection
- No advertising tracking
- Secure encryption protections
- Privacy-first authentication systems

---

## Account Inactivity Policy

Accounts that remain inactive for **4 years** may be permanently deleted to protect system integrity and reduce unused data.
