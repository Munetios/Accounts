About Limited Google Sign-In Support

Munetios is a privacy-first platform. To protect your data, we intentionally avoid one-tap sign-in and embedded Google sign-in methods.

When you choose Sign in with Google, you will be redirected to accounts.google.com. Please be aware that Google may collect telemetry data during this process, as governed by their own privacy policy.
